Tips for Choosing Books for Children

When picking books for children, consider age, interests, and reading level. Illustrated stories like picture books are great for early readers. Encourage curiosity and fun in reading!

How to Choose a Book for Yourself

Pick books that match your interests or challenge you to try something new. Read a few pages first, check the summary, and see if it excites you. Your next favorite read is out there!
